Antispyware software

Software designed to detect, prevent, and remove spyware from computer systems.

Description

Antispyware software is a type of security program specifically designed to identify and eliminate spyware, a category of malicious software that infiltrates a user's device without their consent to collect sensitive information. This information can include personal data, browsing habits, and keystrokes, which can be exploited for identity theft, unauthorized access, or other malicious purposes. Antispyware tools typically work by scanning the system for known spyware signatures, monitoring real-time activity for suspicious behavior, and providing users with options to remove detected threats. They may also include features that help prevent the installation of spyware by blocking suspicious downloads and alerting users to potential threats. Regular updates are crucial for antispyware software to ensure it can protect against new and evolving spyware threats. In a landscape where personal privacy is increasingly at risk, antispyware software plays a vital role in safeguarding users' data and maintaining the integrity of their devices.

  • Spybot Search and Destroy

Additional Information

  • Many antivirus programs include antispyware features.
  • Regular scanning and updates are recommended for effective protection.
